The government thought that this approach gave the criminals too much leeway and decided to put in place a system that would deter criminals more effectively than before. Now, instead of “life in prison”, the criminal is literally incarcerated until they die, with only bread and water for sustenance. Their case is never looked at again, also known as, you have no chance of being released. Other than escaping, that is.
